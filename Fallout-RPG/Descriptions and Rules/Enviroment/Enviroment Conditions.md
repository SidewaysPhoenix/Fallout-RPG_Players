
| Condition              | Effect                                                                                                                               |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Poor Lighting/Darkness | **PER** test that rely on vision and all ranged attacks increase in difficulty by +1, +2, +3, depending on the light level           |
| Mist and Fog           | **PER** tests that rely on vision and all ranged attacks increase in difficulty by +1 when the target is at Medium range or further. |
| Rain                   | Ranged attacks with Energy weapons, and Survival tests, increase in difficulty by +1 or +2, depending how heavy the rain is.         |
| Radstorm               | Any creature not in shelter suffers 2D6 radiation damage at the start of each turn                                                   |
| Extreme Radstorm       | Any creature not in shelter suffers 3D6 radiation damage at the start of each turn                                                   |
| Unusually Sunny        | **PER** test that rely on vision and all ranged attacks increase in difficulty by +1 when the target is at Long range or further.    |
Some conditions affect multiple zones, or even the whole environment. Weather conditions and lighting are the most common examples of this.

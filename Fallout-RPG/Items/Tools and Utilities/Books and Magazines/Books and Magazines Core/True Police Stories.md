True Police Stories was a pre-War pulp magazine boasting “20 pages—a single issue” that was dedicated to editorialized stories on famous criminal investigations and busts of the time. 

* Perk: Once, when you roll the damage of an attack, you may spend a Luck point to turn up to 3 D6 to any face, rather than rolling them. If you learn this perk, you may do this once per scene.

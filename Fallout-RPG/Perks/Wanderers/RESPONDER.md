**Ranks:** 3  

When you succeed at a [[First Aid]] action in combat to stabilize a dying ally, 
* You no longer need to spend 1 AP to wake them from unconsciousness.  
	* In addition, the patient heals 2 HP at the start of their next turn.  


At rank 2, they heal 4 HP at the start of their next two turns.  
At rank 3, they heal 6 HP at the start of their next three turns.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by +7.**

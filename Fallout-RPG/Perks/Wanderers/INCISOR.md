**Ranks:** 2  

When you are wielding a melee weapon,
* The weapon gains the [[Piercing]] 1 damage effect, **or** adds +1 to the rating of any [[Piercing]] X damage effect the weapon already had.  

* At rank 2, this increases to [[Piercing]] 2, **or** adding +2 to any [[Piercing]] X damage effect the weapon already had.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 9.**

**Ranks:** 3  

When you hit an enemy with a melee or ranged attack, 
* You may spend 1 AP to make them vulnerable until the end of their next turn.  
	* While vulnerable, any attack made against that enemy is increased by +1 d6.  


At rank 2, a vulnerable enemy increases damage they receive by +2 d6.  
At rank 3, a vulnerable enemy increases damage they receive by +3 d6.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by +10.**

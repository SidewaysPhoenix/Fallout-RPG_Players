You gain +1 Energy damage resistance against:  

- [[Flamer]]s  
- [[Molotov Cocktail]]s  
- Other fire-based weapons  
- Any weapons with the [[Blast]] quality  

At rank 2, this increases to +2.  
At rank 3, this increases to +3.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 7.**

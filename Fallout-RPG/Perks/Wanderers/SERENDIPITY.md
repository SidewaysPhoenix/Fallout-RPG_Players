**Ranks:** 3  

When your current HP is below one-third of your maximum HP, whenever you are attacked, you may spend 1 Luck point before any dice are rolled to have the attack miss.  

* You may do this a number of times per scene equal to your rank in this perk.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by +6.**

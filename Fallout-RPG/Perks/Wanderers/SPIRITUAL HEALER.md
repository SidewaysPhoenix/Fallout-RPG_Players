**Ranks:** 3  

* When you succeed at a [[First Aid]] action to stabilize a dying ally, you regain 1 HP.  

At rank 2, you regain 2 HP, and a further 2 HP at the start of your next turn.  
At rank 3, you regain 3 HP, and you regain 3 HP at the start of your next two turns.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by +9.**

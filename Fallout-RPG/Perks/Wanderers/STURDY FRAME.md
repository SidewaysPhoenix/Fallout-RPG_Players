**Ranks:** 3  

Armor you are wearing—except for Power Armor—counts as three-quarters of its listed weight.  

* At rank 2, armor worn counts as half its listed weight.  
* At rank 3, armor worn counts as one-quarter its listed weight.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 8.**

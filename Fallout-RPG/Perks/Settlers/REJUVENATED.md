- When you are **full** (the highest hunger state):  
	- Your maximum HP increases by +2.  
	- You may re-roll 1d20 on all **STR**-based skill tests.  
	 - Being full lasts twice as long.  
- When you are **quenched** (the highest thirst state):  
	- You may re-roll 1d20 on all **END**-based skill tests.  
	- You generate one free AP at the start of each of your turns in combat.  
	- Being quenched lasts twice as long.

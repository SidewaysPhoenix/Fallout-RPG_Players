**Ranks:** 2  

- At rank 1, if your hunger state is full or sated, and you have set up a camp with a heat source like a campfire, your **hunger** does not deteriorate.  
- At rank 2, if your thirst state is quenched or hydrated, and you have set up a camp with a heat source like a campfire, your **thirst** does not deteriorate.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 4.**

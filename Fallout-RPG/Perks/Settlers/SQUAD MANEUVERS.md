**Ranks:** 2  

- At rank 1, whenever you travel across the wastes at a hurried pace, you can:  
	 - Maintain that pace for one additional hour by passing a **CHA + Survival** test with a difficulty of 1.  
	 - Gain one further additional hour by spending 2 AP (*for a maximum of 3 hours at a hurried pace*).  
- At rank 2, you can coordinate your allies to join you in combat.  
	 - When you spend 1 AP to take an additional [[Move]] minor action, you can move an ally in the zone you end your movement in to within reach of you by spending another 1 AP.

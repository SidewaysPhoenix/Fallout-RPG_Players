**Ranks:** 3  

Whenever you roll to determine how much money you find, you find more.  

- At rank 1, you find +3D6 caps.  
- At rank 2, you find +6D6 caps.  
- At rank 3, you find +10D6 caps.  

Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 4.

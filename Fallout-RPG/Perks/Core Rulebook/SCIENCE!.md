**Ranks:** 4  

You can modify energy weapons with weapon mods, and you can also craft certain advanced armor mods.  

Each rank in this perk unlocks an additional rank of mods:  
* Rank 1 unlocks rank 1 mods  
* Rank 2 unlocks rank 2 mods  
* Rank 3 unlocks rank 3 mods  
* Rank 4 unlocks rank 4 mods  
**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 4.**

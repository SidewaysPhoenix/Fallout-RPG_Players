**Ranks:** 3  

Whenever you roll to determine how much ammunition you find, you find more.  

- At rank 1, you find +3D6 additional shots.  
- At rank 2, you find +6D6 additional shots.  
- At rank 3, you find +10D6 additional shots.  

The additional ammo you find is the same as initially found.

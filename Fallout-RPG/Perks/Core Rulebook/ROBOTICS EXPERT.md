**Ranks:** 3  

* At rank 1, you can modify robot armor, weapon mounts, and modules with rank 1 mods.  

* At rank 2, you gain access to rank 2 mods, and the difficulty of tests to repair robots is permanently reduced by 1.  

* At rank 3, you gain access to rank 3 mods, and you can reprogram robots to fulfil a different function or alter their behavior at the discretion of the GM. 

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 4.**

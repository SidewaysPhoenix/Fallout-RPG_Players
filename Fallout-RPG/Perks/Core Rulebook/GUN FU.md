### **Requirements**

**Ranks:** 3  

When you succeed at a ranged attack, 
* You may spend 1 AP and 1 ammo to hit a second target within Close range of your initial target. 
	* The second target takes the same damage as the initial target. 
	

At rank 2, you may spend 2 AP and 2 ammo to hit two additional targets.  
At rank 3, you may spend 3 AP and 3 ammo to hit three additional targets.  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 5.**

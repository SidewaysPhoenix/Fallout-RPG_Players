### **Requirements**
Level :: 3+  

STR :: 6  
PER ::   
END ::   
CHA :: 8  
INT ::   
AGI ::   
LCK ::   

**Ranks:** 2  

When you make a **Speech** test to threaten or scare someone, 
* At rank 1, you may re-roll 1d20.  

- At rank 2, you may use a major action in combat to threaten an enemy within Medium range, using a **STR + Speech** test with a difficulty of 2. 
	- If you succeed, that enemy **must** move away from you during their next turn (though they can take any other actions they wish).  

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by +5.**

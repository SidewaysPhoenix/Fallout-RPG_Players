**Ranks:** 3 
**Requirements:** Rare perk, Level 12+ 

When you use the Defend action, and an enemy misses you with a melee attack, you regain 2 HP and add 1 AP to the group pool. 

* At rank 2, increase this to 3 HP and 2 AP. 
* At rank 3, increase this to 4 HP and 3 AP. 

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 6.**

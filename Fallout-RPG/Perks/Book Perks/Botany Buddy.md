**Ranks:** 1 
**Requirements:** Rare book, Level 6+ 

When you forage for food (using the Foraging table on p.202 of the **Fallout: The Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook**), you may spend 1 Luck point. If you do so, then you and your allies (including companions) immediately regain 4 HP add +2 to their Physical damage reduction until the end of the next scene.

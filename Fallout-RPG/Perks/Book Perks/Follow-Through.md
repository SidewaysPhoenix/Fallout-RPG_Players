**Ranks:** 3 
**Requirements:** Rare perk, Level 8+ 

When you succeed at a ranged sneak attack against an enemy, all attacks against that enemy until the start of your next turn inflict +1 D6 damage. 

* At rank 2, this increases to +2 D6. 
* At rank 3, this increases to +3 D6. 

**Each time you take this perk, the level requirement increases by 8.**
